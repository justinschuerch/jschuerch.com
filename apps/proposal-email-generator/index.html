Create proposal emails, client pitch emails, follow-up emails, and final follow-up drafts for freelance and small business work.

How to use it

Enter the client name, service type, project type, budget or price, preferred tone, and any optional note. The tool then generates proposal and follow-up email drafts you can review, edit, and use as a starting point for your own client outreach and project conversations.

Why people use a proposal email generator

Proposal emails and follow-up emails can be repetitive, awkward, and easy to overthink. A proposal email generator helps you move faster, keep your writing professional, and reduce the friction of drafting the same kinds of messages again and again when pitching work or following up with potential clients.

FAQ

Who is this tool for?

It is built for freelancers, consultants, and small business owners who want a faster way to draft proposal emails, pitch emails, and client follow-ups.

What does the tool generate?

It generates proposal email drafts, subject lines, short follow-up emails, warmer follow-up drafts, and final follow-up emails.

Can I use the generated email as-is?

You can use it as a starting point, but it is best to review and adjust the wording so it matches your offer, pricing, tone, and client relationship.

Is this only for freelancers?

No. It can also be useful for agencies, consultants, solo business owners, and other service providers who need cleaner proposal and follow-up emails.

Important note

The tool provides draft email text and subject line suggestions only. Always review and adjust generated proposal and follow-up emails before sending them to a client.
