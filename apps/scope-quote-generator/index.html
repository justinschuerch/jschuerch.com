Why freelancers use a scope and quote generator

Writing scope and quote drafts from scratch can be repetitive, especially when you work on similar client projects over and over. A scope and quote generator helps you move faster, stay more consistent, and communicate project terms more clearly without rebuilding the same structure every time.
