Create client intake questionnaires, discovery questions, and onboarding forms for freelance and small business projects.

Why people use a client questionnaire generator

Client questionnaires are useful, but building them from scratch over and over can be repetitive. A client questionnaire generator helps you move faster, stay more consistent, and ask better questions before the project starts.

FAQ

Who is this tool for?

It is built for freelancers, consultants, agencies, and small business owners who want a faster way to create client intake questionnaires and discovery questions.

What does the tool generate?

It generates an intro message, project goals questions, business questions, content and assets questions, optional design questions, optional technical questions, and a closing message.

Can I use the generated questionnaire as-is?

You can use it as a starting point, but it is best to review and adjust the questions so they match your process, service type, and project requirements.

Is this only for web design projects?

No. It can also be useful for branding, copywriting, marketing, consulting, development, and other client-based service work.

Important note

The tool provides questionnaire drafts and suggested wording only. Always review and adjust the generated questions before sending them to a client.
