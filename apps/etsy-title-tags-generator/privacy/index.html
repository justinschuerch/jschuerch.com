Privacy Policy

Etsy Builder
Effective date: 7 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use Etsy Builder, how that information is used, and how it is stored.

1. What information is collected

Etsy Builder may collect the following information:

  • Your email address, when you enter it for login or purchase access
  • Payment-related information processed by Stripe
  • Basic purchase and access records, such as whether an email has paid access
  • Information you enter into the app, such as product details used to generate listing ideas

2. How your information is used

Your information is used to:

  • Provide access to paid features
  • Process payments and confirm successful purchases
  • Restore access when you log in with the email used for purchase
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app
  • Respond to support or legal requests if needed

3. Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. Etsy Builder does not directly store your full card details. Stripe may collect and process payment information according to its own privacy practices.

4. Data storage

Paid-access email records and related access information may be stored using Supabase or similar backend infrastructure for app functionality and account restoration.

5. Generated content

Information you type into the generator may be processed temporarily to create your results. Please avoid entering highly sensitive personal information into the app.

6. Data sharing

Your information is not sold. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, such as:

  • Stripe, for payment processing
  • Supabase, for data storage and access handling
  • Hosting or infrastructure providers used to run the service

7. Data retention

Email and purchase-related records may be kept for as long as reasonably necessary to provide access, maintain records, prevent abuse, and meet legal or accounting obligations.

8. Your choices

If you would like to request deletion or correction of your email record, you can contact the site owner using the contact information below. Some records may still need to be retained for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or business purposes.

9. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

10. Third-party services

This site may rely on third-party services. Their own policies may also apply when you use their systems.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be posted on this page.

12. Contact
